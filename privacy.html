Overall privacy

This site uses an external api to grab an IP address from your browser and use it to help assign an identifier. It is encoded in base64 then a substring of the base64 is taken so no one can truly know your IP address, but your ip will always have a matching string. Allowing me to index users posts etc. The whole point of this site is to be seemingly anonymous, which is why I do not allow usernames or other fourms of identifing other users.
