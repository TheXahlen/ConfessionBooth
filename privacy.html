Overall privacy

This site uses an external api to grab an IP address from your browser and use it to help assign an identifier. It is encoded in base64 then a substring of the base64 is taken so no one can truly know your IP address, but your ip will always have a matching string. Allowing me to index users posts etc. The whole point of this site is to be seemingly anonymous, which is why I do not allow usernames or other fourms of identifing other users. The exact contents of what are submitted can be seen within a google spreadsheet HERE If for some reason you would like to clear your identifer for a new one or so it cannot be indexed please press the button below. Keep in mind the moment you open the main page index.html it will assign a new identifer. If you have an identifier it will show right below this text. The first {} is the base64 encoded portion of your ip which is also reversed. The second {} is a set of five random identifiers for users who exist on a lan.
